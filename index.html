SenseStreet

Turning smartphones into smart road safety sensors.

SenseStreet detects potholes, road hazards, and crashes using smartphone sensors like accelerometer, gyroscope, and GPS — and maps them in real time to make streets safer.

The Problem

Why SenseStreet Matters

Road accidents and unsafe street conditions remain a major challenge worldwide.

⚠️

Hidden Hazards

Potholes, poor road surfaces, and sudden obstacles often go unreported until they cause accidents or vehicle damage.

📊

Lack of Real-Time Data

Cities lack real-time data about road conditions, making it difficult to respond quickly to dangerous situations.

📱

The Solution Is in Your Pocket

SenseStreet helps solve this problem by crowdsourcing road condition data using the sensors already present in smartphones.

How It Works

Smartphone Sensors Detect Road Conditions

SenseStreet collects and analyzes motion data to identify road anomalies such as potholes, sudden braking zones, and potential crashes. When a hazard is detected, the location is recorded and displayed on a live map.

📐

Accelerometer

Detects sudden bumps and vertical motion changes

🔄

Gyroscope

Measures orientation and rotational movement

📍

GPS

Pinpoints exact hazard locations on the map

⚡

Speed

Monitors velocity changes and sudden braking
