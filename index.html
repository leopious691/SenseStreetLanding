How It Works

Smartphone Sensors Detect Road Conditions

SenseStreet analyzes motion patterns to identify road anomalies such as potholes, sudden braking zones, and potential crashes. Hazards are recorded and displayed on a live map.

📐

Accelerometer

Detects sudden bumps and vertical motion changes

🔄

Gyroscope

Measures orientation and rotational movement

📍

GPS

Pinpoints exact hazard locations on the map

⚡

Speed

Monitors velocity changes and sudden braking

Features
